Use Cases

Invoker empowers European businesses to streamline PEPPOL-compliant invoicing with enterprise-grade features. Here are key scenarios where it shines:

Be Ready to Invoice

Stay ahead of evolving EU e-invoicing mandates (e.g., upcoming requirements in France, Germany, Belgium from 2026). Generate and send fully compliant PEPPOL BIS 3.0 invoices instantly, ensuring regulation-ready formats, structured data for audits, and real-time delivery via the PEPPOL network.

Manage Multiple Companies

Handle invoicing for group structures, subsidiaries, or clients effortlessly in one platform. Centralize operations across entities while maintaining separate compliance profiles, branding, and data isolation.

Customer Management

Build and maintain a robust customer database with PEPPOL IDs, contact details, tax info, and billing histories. Quickly lookup recipients on the network for accurate routing and automate recurring invoices.

Analytics

Gain insights into invoicing performance with dashboards tracking volume, payment timelines, compliance rates, and network delivery status. Spot trends to optimize cash flow.

Workflow

Automate end-to-end invoicing processes: from draft creation and approvals to validation, sending, and status monitoring. Set custom rules for multi-step reviews and notifications.

Experience the serene flow of compliant, automated invoicing—bringing peace of mind and effortless efficiency to your finance operations.
